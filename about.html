Who We Are

Your Trusted Gateway to Sri Lanka

Founded with a deep love for Sri Lanka and its people, Tapro Travels has been crafting unforgettable island experiences for over a decade. We are a team of passionate local travel experts who believe that the best journeys are those that connect you genuinely with a destination — its landscapes, culture, and warmth.

From the misty highlands of Nuwara Eliya to the sun-drenched shores of Mirissa, from the ancient fortresses of Sigiriya to the vibrant streets of Colombo — we know every corner of this extraordinary island, and we are dedicated to sharing it with the world.

  • 100% locally owned and operated
  • Fully customisable itineraries for every traveller
  • 24/7 on-trip support and assistance

Our Mission

To deliver authentic, responsible, and transformative travel experiences across Sri Lanka that inspire travellers, support local communities, and preserve the island's natural and cultural heritage for generations to come.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative Sri Lanka travel specialist globally — renowned for curating journeys that go beyond sightseeing to create deep, meaningful connections between travellers and the Wonder of Asia.

Our Values

Authenticity, sustainability, and genuine hospitality guide everything we do. We partner with local guides, family-run guesthouses, and community-based experiences to ensure your visit benefits the people and places you explore.
