Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Send us a message using the form above.

How far in advance should I book my Sri Lanka trip?

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (December–March). For customised itineraries, 6–8 weeks gives us the best chance to secure your preferred accommodation and guides.

Can I customise a package itinerary?

Absolutely. All our packages are fully customisable. Tell us your interests, travel dates, and budget and we'll build a tailor-made itinerary just for you — from a private honeymoon escape to a family adventure.

What's included in your tour packages?

Most packages include accommodation, transport, an English-speaking guide, and specified meals. Flights are usually excluded. Every package page lists exactly what's included — and we're happy to clarify before you book.

Do you offer airport pick-up and drop-off?

Yes, all our packages include airport transfers from Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB) in Negombo. Our driver will be waiting at arrivals with your name board the moment you land.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. A 20% deposit secures your booking, with the balance due 30 days before departure. We'll walk you through payment when you confirm your trip.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full deposit refund. Cancellations within 30 days may incur charges depending on pre-booked services. We always recommend travel insurance for unexpected situations.
